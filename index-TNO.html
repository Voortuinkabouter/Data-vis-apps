SLS Grapher

Plot a Graph!

Press the Choose Files button and select one or more SLS files (exported as '.txt') to plot the fraction (%) or volume (%) and cumulative (%) on a logaritmic scale. Press the Next and Previous after selecting multiple files to cycle trough the graphs. You can press the Save button to download the plot as '.svg' or press Export to create single Excel file, with a seperate tab for each '.txt' file, containing columns for the diameter, fraction (%)/volume (%), cumulative (%) and other info.
